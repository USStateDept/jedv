Welcome to BIDS!

The Business Information Database System (BIDS) provides U.S. businesses with real-time, on-the-ground leads on international commercial and investment opportunities. The interactive map displays thousands of potential projects, with links to the project website, U.S. Embassies overseas, and other U.S. Government business advocacy resources.

BIDS contains 438 leads across the globe, amounting to a total value of $218,831,779,785.
